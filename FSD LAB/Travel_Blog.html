SWITZERLAND

Switzerland is a postcard-perfect destination where majestic snow-capped mountains meet shimmering blue lakes, offering a unique blend of natural beauty and cultural experiences that are easy to explore via an excellent, punctual train network. From the thrill of adventure activities in the Alps to the charm of quaint villages and vibrant cities, Switzerland provides an unforgettable European journey for every traveler.

  • Ride a cogwheel train to the "Top of Europe"
  • Explore historic castles and old towns
  • Take a scenic cruise on one of Switzerland's many lakes

ITALY

Italy is a breathtaking blend of ancient history, artistic grandeur, and mouthwatering cuisine, a "cultural superpower" that offers "la dolce vita" in its vibrant cities and rolling countryside, though its overwhelming beauty also demands some preparation to fully enjoy its chaotic charm.

  • Take a cooking class to make fresh pasta and tiramisu
  • Explore ancient ruins and historic sites
  • Explore ancient ruins and historic sites

GREECE

From the sun-bleached ancient ruins of Athens to the dazzling white-and-blue villages of the Cycladic islands, Greece is a captivating blend of history, beauty, and unforgettable seaside charm.

  • Explore Athens and its ancient monuments.
  • Go island hopping to discover unique landscapes and beaches.
  • Join a local food or wine tour to taste authentic Greek flavors.
