Paris

Paris, the City of Light, is known for its art, fashion, gastronomy, and culture. Explore charming streets, world-class museums, and iconic landmarks.

  • Visit the Eiffel Tower
  • Explore the Louvre Museum
  • Take a Seine River Cruise

Tokyo

Tokyo blends ultramodern skyscrapers with historic temples. It's a vibrant metropolis full of energy, shopping districts, and culinary delights.

  • Visit the Meiji Shrine
  • Shop in Shibuya and Akihabara
  • Enjoy sushi and ramen
