Dr. A. P. J. Abdul Kalam

Missile Man of India | People's President

1931 – 2015

About Dr. Kalam

Dr. Avul Pakir Jainulabdeen Abdul Kalam was an Indian aerospace scientist and the 11th President of India. He played a crucial role in India's civilian space program and military missile development.

Achievements

  • Known as the "Missile Man of India"
  • Played a key role in Pokhran-II nuclear tests
  • President of India (2002–2007)
  • Recipient of Bharat Ratna

Famous Quote

"Dream, dream, dream. Dreams transform into thoughts and thoughts result in action."
